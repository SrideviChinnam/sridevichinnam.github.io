Working in cross functional teams

Webroas is a glocal digital marketing agency based out of Hyderabad, India. Our name, derived from a marketing metric – ROAS, is short for “Return On Advertising Spend”. We use ROAS to evaluate the overall effectiveness of an advertising campaign. ROAS answers that one marketing question every business has, “If I put [X amount of money] into this marketing channel, what benefits do I get?” Webroas offers local businesses a wide range of services from design to delivery, copywriting to creative solutions, advertising campaigns, branding & beyond. We measure our success on the impact we create and the number of happy customers. Our USP is to produce high-quality emotional branding that is innovative and creative.
