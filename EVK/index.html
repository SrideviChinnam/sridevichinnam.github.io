About

Committed to quality, we at EVK, progressively embrace innovation within the construction industry. We deliver distinction through creativity and expertise to provide multi-dimensional construction solutions. Right from plan to approval and handover, your dream home invariably is our responsibility.

We build assets for you that are grounded in sustainability as social responsibility is our guiding principle. Our quality performance is based upon developing local communities that are embedded in providing both, client and design team satisfaction. We identify value creation opportunities so as to build and nurture world- class assets for you. Underlining our pursuit of excellence is a manifestation of achieving customer delight.
