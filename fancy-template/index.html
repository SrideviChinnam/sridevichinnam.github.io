What's the Catch? Besides More Rewards?

We know it sounds too good to be true. Here's everything you need to know.

Is there really no catch?

Seriously, no catch. We make money through partnerships with brands and property management companies. You get rewards, we get happy users. It's that simple.

How does the cashback work?

Every time you pay rent on time through our platform, you automatically earn 1% cashback. The cashback is deposited into your account within 48 hours of payment confirmation.

Will this affect my credit score?

Only positively! We report your on-time rent payments to major credit bureaus, which helps build your credit history. Late payments are never reported.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security measures. Your payment information is never stored on our servers and is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors.
